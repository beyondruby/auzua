05 / Governance

Rules that protect
everyone including
the founder.

Institutional safeguards that turn entrepreneurial instinct into reliable economic output. Kill-gates, maturity vesting, related-party firewalls all engineered before the first riyal is deployed.

01
Core Engine Maturity-Based Vesting
Performance over tenure. Founders do not earn equity based on the calendar they earn it based on maturity stages: revenue and funding milestones. Prevents the "dead equity" cap tables that sink future rounds.
02
Core Engine Kill-Gate Financial Brake
Automated capital protection. Disbursement auto-stops if break-even cannot be reached within cumulative injection of SAR 4M. Losses are capped by design zombie ventures are eliminated at the contract layer, not the board meeting.
03
Safeguard Unified Representation
Clean cap tables by mandate. Auzua acts as the sole legal representative for all early-stage investors guaranteeing clean cap tables and preventing the fragmentation paralysis that blocks IPOs and major exits.
04
Safeguard Related-Party Firewall
No leakage into side businesses. An automatic shareholder veto triggers on any related-party contract exceeding 15% margin or 9% revenue capital goes to growth, not cousin-ventures.
05
Safeguard Binary Leaver Provisions
Focus, enforced financially. Bad leavers (early exit or breach) are bought out at SAR 1 par value; only good leavers receive fair market value. Early withdrawal becomes economically irrational by design.
